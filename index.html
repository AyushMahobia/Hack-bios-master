ABOUT US

About Us

HACKBIOS is organized by Shri Shankaracharya Technical Campus,Bhilai, Chhattisgarh, India. We had the pleasure of hosting 100+ teams(max 4 per team) in our last offline hackathon. This time we are also targeting school students so that they can be introduced to new technologies and the tech community which will increase their interest in Tech. College students will get hiring opportunities from various companies.

FAQs

FAQs

What if i've never been to a hackathon before?

Perfect! We welcome hackers of all experience levels and backgrounds. We will be providing resources and workshops to help new hackers get started on projects and learn new technologies. There will also be mentors at the event to help guide you.

Can I come with a project pre-built or will I have to build from scratch?

You will be building your project from scratch. However, feel free to come with ideas of what you want to build!

How many people can be on a team?

Up to 4 hackers can be on a team.

What do I need?

An open mind, laptop, charger, and anything else you would need to hack!

Does this event cost money to attend?

No, admission to the event is free! We will also be providing workshops and activities throughout the weekend, as well as virtual swag!

Who can participate?

We hope this event inspires and highlights high school, undergraduate, and graduate students identifying as women or allies to hack. There are also opportunities to participate as mentors and volunteers to support!
